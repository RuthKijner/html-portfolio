The Best Movies To Watch(According to Ruth)

My top 3 movies of all time

Matrix (1999)

A film that was ahead of its time in terms of its amazing effects, while carefully maintaining a suspenseful and interesting plot that draws you completely into the world of Neo. All the high-level actors were carefully chosen, the script is smart and after. For me, the first film in the series is the most successful and always leaves a taste for more

The Lord of the Rings

One of the things that grabs your attention is the shots of breathtaking landscapes in the most beautiful places in the world! When you combine a fascinating story with actors who know how to convey the text at the highest level, this is what makes it possible to watch a 3-hour movie and get bored as soon as it's over.

What Women Want

The film is about a womanizing man who did not take women and vices very seriously. He was cursed with a curse that was also the best gift he could receive in his life began to hear the thoughts of all the women in his life and was terrified to discover everything they thought of him and how they really saw him. But when he began to understand the complexity of women, he learned to love their complexity. Here begins the turning point that makes him a better person, a better father and a better partner. A light-hearted film, elements of romantic comedy. Perfect for a Friday night movie.
